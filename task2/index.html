Tribute to Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace was an English mathematician and writer, chiefly known for her work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. She is considered to be the first computer programmer.

Why I Admire Her

  • First to recognize the full potential of computers.
  • Pioneer in computer science.
  • She broke barriers in a male-dominated field.

Her contributions still inspire generations of programmers and scientists.
